About Me

Hello, I’m Mikaela Porazzo and I am a User Experience Designer. I’m passionate about designing products that delight users.

I started my career as a Customer Success Manager which allowed me to work closely with software users and deeply understand their pain points and goals. From this experience, I was inspired to become a designer so that I could take a more active role in creating user-centric products.


My UX Skills

Research

I use research to make decisions and drive the direction of my project. I develop proto-personas and write open ended questions.

User Empathy

I show empathy by actively listening to users to understand their pains and needs. I then make design decisions that are driven directly from user feedback.

Qualitative Interviews

I conduct in-person interviews and ask open ended questions to gain insights. I continue to ask “why?” to understand motivations and thought processes.
